GOAL

Add a new AI-first module to the MIDDLE COLUMN called:

AI Onboarding & Team Handover

This module must help the sales rep capture only the key commercial and strategic inputs, then use AI to generate:

1. Structured internal handover notes
2. A recommended website sitemap
3. SEO-focused service and location page recommendations
4. Google Ads strategy notes
5. Performance Boost / retargeting notes
6. A structured delivery brief for the head office team
7. A clean “Copy for High Response” output


CORE PRINCIPLE

The rep should NOT need to manually write long handover notes.

The rep should only need to:
- enter client context
- select products sold
- input goals
- upload Ahrefs or keyword data
- provide a few key differentiators and commercial details

Then AI should do the heavy lifting.

The system must feel like:
“Rep enters sales intelligence → AI turns it into production-ready notes.”

--------------------------------------------------
AI ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

When keyword data is uploaded, AI must:

1. Analyse the Ahrefs / keyword data
2. Identify the highest-value keyword opportunities based on:
   - relevance
   - service intent
   - local intent
   - ranking potential
   - commercial intent
3. Recommend the best page structure for SEO
4. Recommend what pages should exist on the website
5. Recommend which service pages and location pages should be prioritised
6. Detect content or structure gaps in the current site
7. Use the client’s business goals to shape the sitemap
8. Produce a practical recommendation, not generic SEO advice

The AI should think like an SEO strategist and delivery lead, not just a summariser.

It should answer:
“What site structure gives this client the best chance of ranking and converting?”
