CALCULATION LOGIC

1. Budget Forecast mode
Estimated Clicks = Net Ad Spend / Avg CPC

Leads Low = Estimated Clicks * CVR Low
Leads Mid = Estimated Clicks * CVR Mid
Leads High = Estimated Clicks * CVR High

CPA Low = Net Ad Spend / Leads Low
CPA Mid = Net Ad Spend / Leads Mid
CPA High = Net Ad Spend / Leads High

Customers Low = Leads Low * Close Rate
Customers Mid = Leads Mid * Close Rate
Customers High = Leads High * Close Rate

Revenue Low = Customers Low * Average Job Value
Revenue Mid = Customers Mid * Average Job Value
Revenue High = Customers High * Average Job Value

Total Investment = Net Ad Spend + Management Fee

ROI Low = Revenue Low / Total Investment
ROI Mid = Revenue Mid / Total Investment
ROI High = Revenue High / Total Investment

2. Market Opportunity mode
Reachable Impressions = Total Monthly Searches * ImpressionShare
Estimated Clicks = Reachable Impressions * CTR

Then continue with the same lead, customer, revenue, and ROI formulas.

3. Break-even
BreakEvenCustomers = TotalInvestment / AverageJobValue
BreakEvenLeads = BreakEvenCustomers / CloseRate

4. Opportunity Score
Build a weighted score out of 100 using:
- demand size
- impression share gap
- seo coverage weakness
- campaign inactive or underdeveloped
- landing page absence
- commercial value / average job value
Keep the logic transparent and maintainable.

VALIDATION RULES
- Avg CPC must be > 0
- Job Value must be > 0 for revenue/ROI
- Close Rate between 0 and 1
- CTR between 0 and 1
- Impression Share between 0 and 1
- CVR Low <= CVR Mid <= CVR High
- Total Monthly Searches >= 0
- Management Fee >= 0
- Net Ad Spend >= 0
