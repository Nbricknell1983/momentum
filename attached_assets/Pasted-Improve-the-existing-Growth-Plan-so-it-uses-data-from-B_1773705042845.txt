HOW AI SHOULD USE CONVERSATION INTELLIGENCE

If conversation notes exist, the AI should prioritise those insights.

Examples:

If the conversation reveals:
- the client wants more leads
- the client is frustrated with marketing
- the client relies on referrals
- the client wants higher quality clients

Then the Growth Plan should frame the strategy around those goals.

Example transformation:

Instead of:

"Your SEO visibility is low."

Say:

"Based on the conversation, the goal is to generate more consistent enquiries. The current digital footprint suggests the business is difficult to discover in several high-intent searches."

The strategy should always connect technical insights to the **client’s stated goals**.

--------------------------------------------------

HOW AI SHOULD USE DEAL INTELLIGENCE

Deal intelligence should influence the tone and focus of the Growth Plan.

Example rules:

If deal stage = Discovery/Meeting
→ focus on opportunity and diagnosis

If deal stage = Proposal
→ focus on roadmap and outcomes

If deal health is low
→ highlight opportunity and urgency

If deal health is strong
→ highlight growth potential

If follow-up is overdue
→ the strategy summary should highlight the most compelling opportunity to re-engage the client.

EXAMPLE USING CURRENT SCREENSHOT DATA

Example Deal Context:

Industry: General Contractor
Location: Brisbane South
Reviews: 5 reviews (4.2)
Website: 17 pages
Ad Spend: $2,500/month
Competitors: Lindon Homes, Besa

AI should interpret this context before generating the Growth Plan.

Example AI reasoning:

Evidence
- 17 pages but 9 are portfolio pages
- only one service page
- no suburb targeting
- competitors have stronger content depth
- Google Ads already active

Interpretation
The business is investing in paid visibility but the website structure limits organic discovery.

Strategic implication
Paid advertising may generate traffic, but long-term visibility and enquiry growth may remain limited without stronger service and location signals.

Recommended move
Strengthen service and suburb visibility while continuing to capture demand through paid campaigns.

WEBSITE MEANING ANALYSIS

The Growth Plan must analyse not just the structure of the website, but what the website is communicating to buyers.

The system should ask internally:

- What problem is the customer trying to solve when they search?
- What message does the website send?
- Who is the website speaking to?
- Does the site help the buyer move forward confidently?

Example:

Portfolio-heavy sites communicate craftsmanship but may under-communicate services and locations.

The AI should highlight this distinction.
