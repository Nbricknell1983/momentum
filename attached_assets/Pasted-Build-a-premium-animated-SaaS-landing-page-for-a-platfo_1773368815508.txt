Build a premium, animated SaaS landing page for a platform called "Momentum".

Momentum is an AI-powered sales, CRM, and growth platform designed for service businesses and sales teams.

The landing page must feel like a high-end SaaS product and be strong enough to immediately sell the concept to prospects.

The tone should be confident, commercial, and conversion-focused.

The main message of the entire page is:

STOP GUESSING. START GROWING.

This line must be the hero headline and repeated strategically throughout the page.

ANIMATION

The site must include premium motion using Framer Motion.
