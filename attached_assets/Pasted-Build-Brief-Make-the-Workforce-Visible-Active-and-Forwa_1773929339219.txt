Build Brief — Make the Workforce Visible, Active, and Forward-Moving
Bullpen still feels too passive.

I do not want a system where the agents sit there waiting for me to press buttons and manually ask them to review things. If these are the workforce, they need to be operating independently and visibly moving Momentum and its clients forward.

Core requirement
When I open Bullpen, I should be able to immediately see:

what each part of the workforce has been reviewing
what they found
what they created or changed
what is blocked or waiting
what needs my attention now
how they are moving the ball forward
I do not want passive review cards that just say “Run Review” or “No review run yet” unless the system genuinely has never been enabled.

The product model
The agents are the workforce.
The workforce should:

wake up on a cadence
review Momentum and client state
generate work
prioritise what matters
surface progress and blockers
only require me for approvals, decisions, and exceptions
Buttons are for override.
Buttons are not the primary operating model.

Required change
For each active review/agent area, the UI needs to show operational motion, not just availability.

Each workforce/review card should show:
What it reviewed
number of leads / clients / work items / systems checked
What it found
top risks
top opportunities
key issues detected
What it did
work items created
items escalated
items updated
priorities changed
What is now waiting
blocked items
awaiting review
approvals needed
unresolved dependencies
What matters next
one clear recommended action or focus area
Example of the standard
I want output that feels like a real operating team, for example:

reviewed 24 active clients, flagged 3 churn-risk accounts, created 2 intervention items, 1 account needs immediate attention
reviewed 41 active leads, found 6 stalled in stage, created 4 follow-up actions, 2 require urgent sales movement
reviewed operational health, detected 1 broken integration, escalated 1 blocker, 2 queue items remain unresolved
That is the level of visible motion I want.

Required behavior
once scheduling is enabled, reviews should run automatically
latest results should be visible by default
the cards should not sit empty waiting for me to initiate everything
I should be able to see what changed since the last run
no fabricated urgency if nothing changed, but also no dead-looking cards if the workforce has in fact been active
Important distinction
I do not want generic activity theatre.

I want:

real reviewed scope
real findings
real work created
real blockers
real next actions
Everything shown should come from actual system/client/work state.

Scope
This should apply across:

Momentum operational health
client review activity
pipeline review activity
any other workforce areas already running on schedule
Success criteria
This is successful when Bullpen stops feeling like:

a page of buttons and cards
and starts feeling like:

a real team that has already been at work before I arrived.
When I open Bullpen, I should instantly understand:

what the workforce did
what changed
what matters most now
where I need to step in
