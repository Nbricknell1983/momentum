I want the agents to consistently produce a full first-pass intelligence pack for leads, similar to what they generated for First Parking.

Problem:
Some leads still do not feel like they are getting a complete Deal Intelligence output, even though First Parking shows the intended standard:
- Presence Snapshot
- Customer Profile
- Search Intent
- Website Interpretation
- Opportunities
- Key Gaps
- Commercial Angle
- Call Priorities
- Discovery Questions

Goal:
Make this richer intelligence pack the default behaviour for viable leads, not the exception.

Product rule:
If a lead has enough baseline information (business name, website, location, plus any early evidence), the agent should generate a useful first-pass intelligence pack even if some deeper signals are still missing.

What I want:
1. Support a minimum viable intelligence pack mode
2. Generate the best available first-pass intelligence from partial evidence
3. Progressively enrich that pack as deeper specialist outputs arrive
4. Avoid all-or-nothing behaviour where sparse evidence leads end up with weak or missing intelligence

Desired intelligence pack structure:
- Presence Snapshot
- Customer Profile
- Search Intent
- Website Interpretation
- Opportunities
- Key Gaps
- Commercial Angle
- Call Priorities
- Discovery Questions

Implementation guidance:
1. Identify where the Agent Intelligence / prep pack synthesis prompt is currently gated
2. Relax hard dependencies so a first-pass pack can still generate from partial evidence
3. Introduce a “minimum viable intelligence pack” path for leads with:
- business name
- website or domain
- location/suburb
- any early presence/search/GBP/website evidence
4. Allow sections to degrade gracefully when evidence is thin:
- shorter customer profile
- likely search intent instead of highly specific intent
- provisional opportunities/gaps
- fewer discovery questions
5. Continue upgrading the pack automatically as richer evidence arrives

Important UX rule:
Do not make the pack feel empty or withheld just because some signals are incomplete.
It is better to show a lighter but coherent first-pass pack than to wait for perfect evidence.

Success criteria:
- more leads produce a Deal Intelligence pack shaped like the First Parking example
- sparse-evidence leads still get a useful first-pass intelligence view
- the intelligence pack progressively improves as more specialist outputs arrive
- the system feels consistently proactive and insightful across leads, not only on the strongest examples

Please identify:
1. what minimum evidence threshold should unlock first-pass intelligence generation
2. which current gating rules are too strict
3. the smallest safe refactor to support a minimum viable intelligence pack
4. how to gracefully degrade each intelligence section when evidence is partial
5. how to preserve progressive enrichment without regressions to the stronger full-pack cases
