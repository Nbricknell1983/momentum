- call intent

- opening angle
- question plan
- objection predictions
- close strategy
- allowed tools/actions

- let Vapi decide the strategy without the Momentum call brief
- let Erica use tools outside the allowed actions
- let Erica hard close unnaturally
- let Erica ignore source context
